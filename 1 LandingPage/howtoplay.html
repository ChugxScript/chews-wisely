As a Host:

1. Check the waiting room to see who wants to join the vote.

2. Approve or reject users by clicking on their username. Avoid duplicates in the same room.

3. Use the check icon to accept and the remove (x) icon to decline.

4. View the submitted options for the current vote.

5. Delete or remove choices if necessary.

6. Always keep at least one option for a smooth voting session.

As a Player:

1. Check the options available.

2. Hover over your preferred option and click to select it as your top choice (Rank 1).

4. To change your vote, click on a different option to update your preferences.

5. Click "Submit" when you're happy with your rankings.
